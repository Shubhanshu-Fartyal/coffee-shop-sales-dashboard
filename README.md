# Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze coffee shop sales performance. It provides insights into revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance to support business decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Coffee shop owners need to understand sales patterns, customer demand, and product popularity to improve inventory planning, staffing, and promotional strategies.

This dashboard helps answer those questions using transaction-level sales data.

---

## Business Questions Answered

### Overall Performance

- What is the total revenue generated?
- How many transactions were completed?
- What is the average order value?

### Sales Trends

- How do sales change across different months?
- Which months perform the best?

### Product Analysis

- Which coffee products generate the highest sales?
- Which products contribute the least?

### Customer Behavior

- During which time of day are customers most active?
- How is customer demand distributed throughout the day?

---

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Product-wise Sales Analysis
- Sales by Time of Day
- Interactive Dashboard Design

---

## Dataset

- 3,636 Sales Transactions
- Coffee Products
- Sales Amount
- Date & Time
- Payment Type

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Visualization

---

## Key Insights

- Total sales exceeded **115K** across **3,636** transactions.
- Afternoon sales slightly outperformed morning and night sales.
- Latte emerged as the highest-selling product.
- Monthly sales showed clear fluctuations, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
